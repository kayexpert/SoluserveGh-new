who we are

SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED is a 100% locally owned Ghanaian Company. We are registered with the Ghana Petroleum Commission to provide services to the Oil and Gas sector in Ghana. The company has built capacity through the development of a robust Well Operations Management System, Safety Management System and Quality Control and Assurance System. The company’s management systems and procedures have been audited and an Integrated Management System Certification, (ISO 9001: 2015 for Quality Management System; ISO 14001:2015 for Environmental Management System; and ISO 45001:2018 for Occupational Health & Safety System) has been issued to guide its operations. The services we provide are in four (4) distinct categories.

  • Drilling Engineering and Drilling related Services
  • Well Services, Well Integrity and Intervention and Production related Services
  • Oil and Gas Training Services
  • Shorebase and Logistics Management Services

Our mission

Our mission is to become an industry leader in the provision of varied well operations & intervention services and well operations & engineering management by providing a cost effective, quality engineered solution to our clients.

joint venture partnership

SGS SOLUSERV LIMITED - Soluserv (GH) Ltd. is in a Joint Venture (JV) partnership with SGS Ghana Limited to provide laboratory analytical services of samples, minerals services and environmental sampling support to the Mining sector in Ghana. The JV also provides technical support with regards to other types of environmental samples such as soil, noise, vegetation, agricultural crops, fish and air quality monitoring filters.
